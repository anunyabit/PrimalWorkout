Primal Fitness Program

Monday – Full-Body Strength
  • Dynamic Warm-Up – 7 min
  • Barbell Back Squat – 4x6 @ 80% 1RM
  • Sandbag Overhead Press – 3x8
  • Barbell Bent-Over Row – 3x10
  • Twisting Sandbag Lunge – 3x8/side
  • Static Stretch Cooldown – 5 min
Tuesday – Mobility & Flexibility
  • Deep squat hold – 2x1 min
  • Shoulder dislocates with band – 2x10
  • Cat-cow stretch – 2x10
  • World’s Greatest Stretch – 2x/side
Wednesday – Functional Strength
  • Dynamic Warm-Up – 7 min
  • Deadlift – 4x5 @ 80%
  • Sandbag Bear Hug Squats – 3x12
  • Band-Resisted Pushups – 3x10
  • Hanging Leg Raises – 3x10
  • Loaded Carry (Sandbag) – 3x30 sec
Thursday – Mobility & Flexibility
  • Jefferson curl (light weight) – 2x10
  • Hip flexor stretch – 2x30 sec/side
  • Thoracic bridge – 2x10
  • Shoulder circles – 2x15
Friday – Strength & Conditioning
  • Dynamic Warm-Up – 7 min
  • Barbell Front Squat – 4x6
  • Pull-ups or Band Pull-downs – 3x10
  • Sandbag Rotational Clean – 3x8/side
  • Side Plank with Reach Under – 3x10/side
  • Cooldown: Forward fold
